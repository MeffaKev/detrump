# Privacy Policy for DeTrump

*Last updated: June 13, 2026*

## Overview

DeTrump is a browser extension that blurs images of Donald Trump on web pages. This policy describes what data the extension does and does not collect.

## Data Collection

DeTrump does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No information about you, your browsing activity, or the pages you visit is ever sent to any server — by us or any third party.

## Local Storage

The extension stores a single preference (`detrump_enabled`: true or false) in your browser's local storage. This value records whether you have toggled the extension on or off. It never leaves your device.

## Permissions

DeTrump requests access to all URLs so it can scan page content for images to blur. This access is used solely to inspect image attributes (such as `alt` text and filenames) locally in your browser. No page content or browsing history is recorded or transmitted.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of the extension, the updated policy will be published here and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can open an issue at the extension's GitHub repository.
